Welcome to the Online Examination portal of INDIAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY,(BHU),VARANASI
Before proceeding to the Examination,
Read the following guidelines as given below ::

  • Step 1:Click on "Sign-Up" if not signed in
                else click on "Login" if already signed in

  • Step 2:If there is a new "Sign-Up" fill up all your
                details (For e.g.:Name ,Email ,Contact
                ,Department ,Semester) and click on "Submit"

  • Step 3:A confirmation mail will be sent to your
                E-mail address you gave and enter
                6-digit confirmation Code and click "confirm"

  • Step 4:After confirming, you will be logged in
                our database

  • Step 5:Now ,log into your exam page
                and select your exam type
